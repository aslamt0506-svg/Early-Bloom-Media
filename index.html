FAQ

Quick answers for busy directors.

If you have strict photo policies, limited time, or multiple classrooms—this is built for you.

Do you need photos of children? +
Not necessarily. We can create strong content using classroom setups, hands-on activities, teacher spotlights, curriculum graphics, facility tours, and permission-safe moments.
How much time will this take from our team? +
Typically 20–30 minutes per month for approvals. If you send occasional photo/video snippets, we handle the rest.
Can you match our brand and tone? +
Yes—warm, professional, playful, Montessori-inspired, faith-based, bilingual, or academic-focused. We align to your center’s voice and parent expectations.
What platforms do you manage? +
Most centers focus on Facebook + Instagram. We can also support Google Business Profile posts and Nextdoor-style community messaging as add-ons.
Do you run paid ads? +
We can prepare ad-ready creative and a boosting plan. If you want full ad management, we’ll include it as a custom add-on based on your location and competition.
Is this month-to-month? +
Yes. Start month-to-month. If you love it, we can move to a 3–6 month plan with bonus content.
